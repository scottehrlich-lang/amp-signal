What it is
AMP Signal makes every Sinclair seller — regardless of experience, market size, or role — operate with the intelligence, focus, and strategic clarity of a 20-year media veteran with a team of analysts working for them around the clock.
01 · Principle
Bespoke over broadcast.
No two users see the same view. Every surface is built for that person's role, market, and accounts.
02 · Principle
Push over pull.
Intelligence comes to the user, not the other way around. A briefing — not a search engine.
03 · Principle
Agents do the work.
Specialized agents — not one model. Each owns a domain. Users see output, not machinery.
04 · Principle
Clarity at every level.
Where do I stand? What needs me now? What's my move? Three answers, every view, every role.
