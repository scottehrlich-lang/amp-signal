Our promise

Receipts. Records. Real people.

We've been here 29 years because we do right by people, even on the days something goes wrong. Here's exactly how we work.

  1. A printed receipt for every wash. Every transaction generates a receipt with date, time, plate, and tier — kept on file for 90 days.
  2. The price you see is the price you pay. No quiet upgrades, no hidden surcharges. The kiosk shows the same number the sign shows.
  3. If we damage it, we own it. Walk into the office, show your receipt, and we'll review the bay's video and respond within 48 hours.
  4. Family-owned, accountable in person. Sonny is here most days. If something is off, ask for him directly.
